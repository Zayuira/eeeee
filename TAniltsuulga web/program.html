-Make a website(beginner)

The "Girls Code Program 2022" was aimed at reducing gender inequality in the telecommunications and information technology sector, closing regional digital divide, and training human resources for the sector. The program was initiated by B.Bolor-Erdene, State Secretary of the Ministry of Digital Development and Communications, and is being organized for the second year with the support of ONDO LLC, ICT Group, Tapatrip, Unitel, Skytel, Khan Bank, Golomt Bank, and Information and Communication Network. In total, more than 1,500 girls from all over Mongolia registered and 50 girls were selected.
